How to properly prepare the file?


In order for the printout to achieve the highest quality, it is good to prepare the file according to the following guidelines:


  1. resolution: 300dpi
  2. color space: CMYK
  3. print size: 57x100cm
  4. extension: PDF
  5. transparency, if the print requires it
  6. without mirror reflection

The printout should not contain elements smaller/thinner than 1mm.


In the case of such small patterns, the following may occur:


  1. incomplete coverage of the adhesive and, consequently, the impossibility of applying these areas
  2. delicate, white halo extending beyond the printout (white underprint)

To minimize the risk of making a mistake - we have prepared templates in the most popular extensions, to be downloaded from the home page.

Acceptable file extensions: JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF, TIFF
